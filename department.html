What do you learn in a Management Information Systems (MIS) college?

Let me explain simply:

In an MIS college, you learn how to use technology to solve business problems. Here are the main things you usually study AND How computers work hardware and software basics

  • Databases (how to store and organize data)
  • Networking (how computers connect and share information)
  • Programming (writing simple programs, usually languages like Java, or C++)
  • Business basics (management, marketing, accounting)
  • Systems analysis and design (how to plan and create information systems)
  • Project management (how to lead technology projects)
  • Web development (making websites and online systems)
